Str8UpDriven LLC is headquartered in Stanton, California. Founded in 2021 by Tony Nino, the company operates as a wholesale automotive mentorship network and dealer co-op.

FAQ

Common questions

How does the funding work?

−

Inside the Membership, you'll learn how to source and evaluate deals the same way our team does. Every deal goes through the same underwriting Tony uses on his own capital. When it qualifies, you get funded to execute it and split profits per program terms. Nothing is approved on the spot without review.

When are the live calls?

+

Two times per week: Tuesday and Thursday. Tony reviews real deals, answers questions, and gives you your next steps.

What are the 3 tools included?

+

You'll get software to help you find cars, evaluate true value, and track your pipeline. Tool access is included with active Membership.

Can I cancel?

+

Yes, cancel anytime, no long-term contract. Once you do, you'll lose access to the mentorship content, live calls, and the ability to use Tony's capital for funded deals going forward. Most members stay because the program keeps paying for itself, not because they're locked in.
